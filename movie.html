⭐6.1/10 ☆ Rate ╋ Watchlist

In theaters Dec. 5

A FNAF sequel was inevitable after the f irst movie became the highest grossing horror film of 2023. This time around, terrifying events are set in motion when Abby tries to reconnect with her animatronic friends, unleashing a long-forgotten horror. Expect this second outing to expand on the first film's lore, although we're hoping for a nod to the second game via some kind of prequel subplot (c'mon, Yello Rabbit!). Josh Hutcherson, Elizabeth Lail, Piper Rubio, and Matthew Lillard reprise their roles, plus horror heartthrob Skeet Ulrich and Zoomer icon Mckenna Grace are joining in on the Freddy’s fun. But will it be scary? Matthew Lillard thinks so, saying in a recent interview that the sequel is more action-packed, “amped up,” and will feature more jump scares. Hold onto your popcorn. — Adrienne
